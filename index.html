THE MAN BEHIND THE BRAND

Based in the vibrant heart of Las Vegas, Jonas Evans is a multi-dimensional creator and strategic entrepreneur. As V3GASBOI, he has redefined independent music production by blending high-energy soundscapes with cutting-edge AI-generated visuals. In 2025, his influence expanded across 35 countries, amassing over 4,000 global plays and solidifying his place in the international digital music scene.

Beyond the studio, Jonas manages a growing e-commerce empire, including the Vegas Spot and Fashions to You boutiques. This synergy between high-end fashion and urban production creates a lifestyle brand that resonates from the Las Vegas Strip to listeners worldwide. He continues to push the boundaries of what it means to be a modern mogul, cooking for his community while building a global digital legacy.
