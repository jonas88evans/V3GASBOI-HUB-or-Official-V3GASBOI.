The Evolution of V3GASBOI

Established in the heart of Las Vegas, Jonas Evans is more than a producer—he is a strategic visionary. Operating under the alias V3GASBOI, he has successfully merged the raw energy of urban production with the limitless possibilities of AI-driven creative content.

In 2025 alone, Jonas reached listeners in over 35 countries, amassing more than 4,000 global plays. This international growth reflects his commitment to high-end sound and innovative visuals, like those featured in his tracks "Turn Me Into A Monster" and "Im Bipolar".

Jonas's entrepreneurial drive extends into the world of e-commerce, where he manages the Vegas Spot and Fashions to You boutiques. From preparing large-scale holiday meals for his community to building a global digital brand, his work defines the modern Las Vegas hustle.
