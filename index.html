From Streets to Studio — A Story of Redemption

Jonas Evans · Raw hip-hop · One vote away from working with Timbaland

V3GASBOI (Jonas Evans) isn't chasing fame — he's rewriting his destiny. The Las Vegas native turned his pain into rhythm, his past into poetry. After jail at 18, addiction, and raising two kids as a teen father, he launched his solo career in July 2024. Now in March 2025, he stands at the threshold of a life-changing opportunity: a chance to produce with the legendary Timbaland.

🔹 Father at 15 🔹 Incarcerated at 18 🔹 Clean & focused 🔹 Lyrical Mindz founder

📖 The Backstory: Survival Over Spectacle

Growing up without a father, Jonas learned the hard way. At 15 he became a dad, determined to break the cycle. But the streets of Vegas pulled him in — by 18 he was locked up in Las Vegas County Jail. That wake-up call, followed by a battle with substance abuse, nearly swallowed him whole.

The turnaround? Love and music. He met his wife Dayliset Evans at 24, built a family, and together with his brother formed the group Lyrical Mindz. Every bar he writes today is a confession: from rock bottom to the recording booth.

🎧 The 2025 Timbaland Contest

In March 2025, V3GASBOI entered a high-stakes competition for a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity: co-produce a track with Timbaland. For an independent Vegas artist, this could change everything — amplifying his raw, unfiltered storytelling to a global audience. He’s rallying his fanbase to vote daily, share his story, and prove that second chances deserve the biggest stages.

“This isn't performative rap. This is a guy who crawled back from addiction to provide for his kids. His music is therapy.” — V3GASBOI's mission
